I am a detail-oriented researcher, engineer, inventor, consultant, and data analyst. Skilled in collecting, analyzing, interpreting, and reporting different types of structured and unstructured data. Experienced writing and proofreading technical documents, creating visualizations, designing and configuring software solutions, providing technical support, and working independently and collaboratively to achieve objectives.

I have a Ph.D. in the Computer Engineering from the University of California, Santa Cruz. I researched and developed a <a href="https://escholarship.org/uc/item/6nq496gw">robust path back-tracing guidance system</a> to help blind or visually impaired people retrace the path taken in indoor environments. I also developed a system to convey real-time travel-related information when blind or visually impaired people use public transportation, and an openly accessible and annotated daseset of inertial sensor time-series data collected from blind individuals walking through long and complex paths in realistic conditions. 

I worked at IBM Research as a Research Staff Member spearheading sensor-based research projects to develop Cloud-based & on-premise enterprise software solutions. Some of the sensors and devices I worked with include wireless sensors (Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) beacons, Ultra-Wide Band (UWB) transponders, Wi-Fi routers), camera sensors (RGB and Time-of-Flight (ToF) cameras), MEMS inertial sensors (accelerometers, magnetometers, and gyroscopes), and other Internet of Things (IoT) computing devices. I conducted experiments to study the properties and characteristics of these sensors, developed time-series and lightweight deep learning models, and applied their usability to use cases including, but not limited to, indoor and outdoor navigation systems, room occupancy detection, asset tracking, and biometrics authentication for autonomous vehicles.

I completed a combined total of 6 internships at Intel (2), Volcano (1), NASA (2), and IBM Research (1). I am passionate about conducting research and using technology to develop solutions with the purpose of helping others and our environment. I advice and assist users with computer inquiries and issues, and I have been learning about Geographic Information System (GIS).
